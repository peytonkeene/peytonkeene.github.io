About MedNarrate

Designed by EMS experience, built for better documentation workflows.

MedNarrate was created to reduce documentation burden for providers while improving the consistency, clarity, and completeness of patient care narratives across agencies.

Our Mission

Helping providers spend more time on patient care and less time on paperwork.

MedNarrate is a leading provider of narrative generation solutions for EMS providers. Our mission is to streamline the documentation process, enabling healthcare professionals to focus more on patient care and less on paperwork.

Founded by Peyton Keene, a paramedic and website developer, MedNarrate understands the unique challenges EMS providers face. Our platform is designed to reduce the time spent on documentation while ensuring that narratives are thorough, accurate, and compliant with industry standards.

With MedNarrate, EMS teams can quickly generate narratives that capture all essential details, improving both the quality of patient care and the efficiency of operations. Our commitment to innovation and user-friendly design makes MedNarrate the go-to solution for EMS providers nationwide.

Join us in revolutionizing EMS documentation and discover how MedNarrate can help your team work smarter, not harder.
